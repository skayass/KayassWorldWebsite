Borek

Borek was a true favorite of the Ottoman Empire. Its name is believed to be derived from the Turkish word bur, ‘to twist’.And the first thing you’ll notice about this flavor-filled baked pastry is its twisting, spiral shape.Borek is traditionally made with filo or yufka pastry. It’s baked in a large pan and then cut into slices once ready.
