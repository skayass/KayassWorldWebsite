Baby Type Meal(4 to 6 Months)

In general, fruits are introduced 1 to 2 weeks after vegetables, because they are more easily accepted by babies with their sweet taste. They provide plenty of vitamins, minerals and fiber. At first, they are offered cooked, stewed. When baby grows up and begins to chew, from 6-7 months, we can offer him very ripe raw fruit, crushed or in thin slices, or even in melting pieces that baby can grab to eat on his own.
